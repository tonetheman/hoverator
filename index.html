Hoverator

The left and right arrow keys control the engines on each side of the hovercraft.

Hover by holding the left and right arrow keys together.

To turn in a direction, briefly release the other key (e.g., to lean right, briefly take your finger off the left arrow key).

Hard mode adds a box that you have to carry to the end without dropping.

Press the down arrow to restart if you're stuck. Good luck!
